Mission Statement

We honour the memory of those lost in the Grenfell Tower tragedy by creating a space of remembrance, healing, and community empowerment. Your support will help build a meaningful and lasting memorial that reflects resilience, justice, and unity.

Plans and Timeline

  • Phase 1: Memorial design and community consultations – Q1 2026
  • Phase 2: Planning permission and contractor procurement – Q2-Q3 2026
  • Phase 3: Construction and installation – Q4 2026
  • Unveiling: June 2027
